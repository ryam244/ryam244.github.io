よくある質問 / FAQ

Q. 遊び方が分かりません。 / How do I play?
A. アプリ内の「遊び方（How to Play）」と、初回のチュートリアルで基本ルールを確認できます。各カードの「数字」と「マーク」を推理し、Hit（位置も一致）/ Blow（含まれるが位置違い）の手がかりで正解の列を当てます。
The in-app “How to Play” screen and the first-run tutorial explain the rules: deduce each card’s number and suit using the Hit (right spot) / Blow (wrong spot) feedback.

Q. 記録やデータが消えました。 / I lost my records.
A. 記録・設定は端末内のみに保存されます（外部送信なし）。アプリを削除すると記録も消えますのでご注意ください。問題が続く場合は上記メールへご連絡ください。
Records and settings are stored only on your device (never uploaded). Deleting the app also deletes them. If problems persist, email us above.

Q. 広告について / About ads
A. 本アプリは基本無料で、任意のインタースティシャル広告が表示されます。iOS ではトラッキング許可（ATT）に応じてパーソナライズ/非パーソナライズが切り替わります。
MarkBlow is free with optional interstitial ads. On iOS, personalized vs. non-personalized ads depend on your App Tracking Transparency choice.

5. 第三者への提供

本アプリは、利用者の情報を第三者に提供することはありません。

6. データの削除

端末内に保存された情報は、本アプリのアンインストール、または設定画面の「記録のリセット」により削除できます。

7. 子どものプライバシー

本アプリは個人情報を収集しないため、お子様にも安心してご利用いただけます。

Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how the "MarkBlow" app ("the App") handles user information.

1. Collection of Personal Information

The App does not collect any personal information such as your name, email address, or location. No registration or account creation is required.

2. Information Stored on the Device

The App stores game settings (such as difficulty) and best records only on your device. This information is never sent to any external server.

3. Network Communication

Gameplay itself runs fully offline, and your play data and records are never sent externally. Network communication occurs only to deliver advertisements, as described below.

4. Advertising

For certain single-player features (such as extending the number of guesses), the App may display advertisements (e.g. Google AdMob interstitial ads) that the user voluntarily chooses to watch. Ad SDKs may process information such as advertising identifiers for ad delivery, fraud prevention, and frequency capping. On iOS, the App requests App Tracking Transparency (ATT) permission; if you decline, you will see non-personalized ads. Watching ads is never mandatory.

5. Disclosure to Third Parties

The App does not provide user information to any third party.

6. Deleting Data

Information stored on the device can be deleted by uninstalling the App or by using "Reset records" on the settings screen.

7. Children's Privacy

Because the App collects no personal information, it can be used safely by children.

8. Changes to This Policy

This Policy may be revised as needed. The revised Policy takes effect once posted within the App.

Traditional Moon Names

Discover tonight's moon by its poetic Japanese name — Mikazuki, Jūsan-ya, Mochizuki, Izayoi, Tachimachi-zuki and more.

Full Japanese lunar date in the Reiwa era, traditional month names (Mutsuki–Shiwasu), 24 solar terms, and 72 micro-seasons.
